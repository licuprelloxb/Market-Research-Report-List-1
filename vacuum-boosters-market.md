<p><strong>The Vacuum Boosters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-sealed Pump</li><li>Water-ring Pump</li><li>Dry Vacuum Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum boosters market encompasses various types, including oil-sealed pumps, water-ring pumps, and dry vacuum pumps. Oil-sealed pumps utilize a lubricating oil to create a vacuum, offering high efficiency and reliability in various industrial applications. Water-ring pumps employ water as a sealing fluid, providing consistent performance and ease of maintenance, making them suitable for handling vapors and condensable gases. Dry vacuum pumps operate without lubricants, minimizing contamination risks, and are ideal for clean processes in pharmaceuticals and food industries.</p></p>
