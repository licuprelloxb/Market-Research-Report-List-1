<p><p>The dry scroll vacuum pumps market is primarily segmented into two types: single-sided scroll and double-sided scroll pumps. Single-sided scroll pumps utilize one set of scrolls to compress and transfer gas, offering simplicity and lower maintenance. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps employ two sets of interleaved scrolls, providing higher pumping efficiency and greater gas throughput. These variations cater to different industrial applications based on required vacuum levels, operational efficiency, and space constraints, enhancing their utility across various sectors.</p></p>
